# Ferry Management System

A backend-driven system designed to manage transportation scheduling, tracking, and notifications for ferry or shuttle services. The system aims to streamline operations for administrators while improving convenience for users.

---

## Overview

The Ferry Management System provides a centralized platform to handle:

- Route and schedule management  
- Passenger or student tracking  
- Notification of activities and absences  
- Basic transaction handling  

It is designed to improve operational efficiency and provide better visibility for both service providers and users.

---

## Objectives

- Simplify scheduling and route management  
- Enable tracking and status updates  
- Provide notifications for important events  
- Improve coordination between operators and users  

---

## Problem Statement

Traditional transport systems often face:

- Manual and inefficient scheduling processes  
- Lack of visibility for users  
- Difficulty in tracking usage and attendance  
- Poor communication between operators and users  

---

## Solution

This system introduces:

- Centralized scheduling for routes and time slots  
- Tracking mechanism for monitoring usage and activities  
- Notification system for updates and alerts  
- Basic transaction handling for operational workflows
